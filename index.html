Towing

No matter how your car sits — front-wheel, rear-wheel, or all-wheel drive — we have the right equipment to get it safely moved. Our rates are affordable, but our methods match the quality of big-name towing services.

Jump Start

Whether you’re at home, work, or on the road, our jump start service runs $45–$65 based on location. If the jump doesn’t solve the issue, the cost rolls into your tow — unless you decline towing.

Lock out

Locked out of your vehicle? It happens to the best of us. Our unlock service ranges from $60 to $85 depending on the complexity — including factors like vehicle type (Tesla, convertible) or risk of damage. We're fast, careful, and always up front about the price.

Tire change

Tire changes range from $60 to $100. Please ensure you have a usable spare, a wheel lock key (if needed), and a spare rod for trucks or SUVs. If we’re unable to complete the job, your payment will apply toward a tow — no extra charge for the tire attempt.

About Us

Good News Towing is a family-run business proudly serving Johnson County and the surrounding areas. We combine affordable pricing with dependable service — treating every call like it matters, because it does. Beyond towing, we’re committed to giving back, supporting charities like City Union Mission, Joni and Friends, and the Jared Coones Pumpkin Run. Whether you need a jump, tow, or tire change, we’re here to help — with integrity, care, and a smile.
